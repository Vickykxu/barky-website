Billy the Pig

Meet Billy the Pig

Billy the Pig is Barky's archnemesis. They met in Kindergarden, where Billy started a rumor that Barky's fur had fleas. The whole class made fun of Barky and kept their distance away from him. As a result of the rumor, Barky has been unable to make any friends. From that day, the two have been clashing and trying to make the other look foolish.

Some of his hobbies include:

  • Spreading rumors about Barky
  • Trying to steal Johnson away from Barky
  • Dancing (He is the best dancer at their school)
  • Playing soccer (He is the team captain and MVP)

Sally the Sheep

Meet Sally the Sheep

Sally was the first person that took an interest in Barky. They dated for a week before Billy's rumor about Barky having fleas took place. Sally was disgusted at Barky and gave him the nickname "Stink".

Some of her hobbies include:

  • Making fun of other people in class
  • Teacher's pet
  • Painting (It's bad though)
  • Swimming (Team captain)
